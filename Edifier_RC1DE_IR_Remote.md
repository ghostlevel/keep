# Edifier 'RC1DE' IR remote codes used for Edifier R1280DB Speakers


IR Protocol identified as 8E7807F -> NEC


- Power: 8E7807F
- Mute: 8E700FF
- volume down: 8E730CF
- volume up: 8E7906F
- Line 1: 8E750AF
- Line 2: 8E7A857
- OPT: 8E7B04F
- COX: 8E76897
- BT: 8E7708F
- Play/Pause: 8E728D7
- next: 8E710EF
- previous: 8E7609F
